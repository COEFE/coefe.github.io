About Us

Discover the story behind GolfBelly

GolfBelly is a global community of passionate private club golfers who love to share the game with others. Our members believe that the golf experience is as much about the “who with” as it is about the “where at”. They want to share their club and experience with others while also having the same personal experience in their own golf travels.

It was started in 2017 by Manish Goel, a retired high-tech corporate executive and an avid golfer, as a passion project to easily connect with other like-minded golfers. Today GolfBelly is a 11000+ strong and growing community of people from 3200+ clubs in 40 countries, including 84 clubs in the world top 100. Our members love the game, cherish the opportunity to meet new golfers, and experience the best the golf world has to offer.
